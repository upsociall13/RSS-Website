Our Journey

A Century of Purpose,
Discipline & Devotion

1925
Foundation at Nagpur
Dr. Keshav Baliram Hedgewar establishes the Sangh on Vijayadashami — a movement rooted in discipline, culture, and national pride. The first shakha begins with a handful of dedicated youth.
1947 – 1970s
Post-independence expansion
Shakhas spread across every state. Relief work during partition, floods, and earthquakes establishes RSS as a national first-responder force. Guruji Golwalkar steers the organisation through transformative decades.
1980s – 2000s
Social transformation at scale
Expansion into education, tribal welfare, rural development, and cultural preservation through 36 affiliated organisations. Sewa Bharati becomes one of India's largest volunteer networks.
Today → 2047
Viksit Bharat Vision
Advancing towards a developed, united, and self-reliant India for the nation's centenary of independence — building a civilisational powerhouse from the grassroots up.
🕉
Cultural Integrity
Preserving Bharatiya traditions, values, and civilisational heritage so future generations inherit an unbroken legacy.
🤝
Social Harmony
Bridging caste, class, and regional divides through the bond of shared national identity and mutual respect.
📚
Character Building
Developing disciplined, patriotic citizens through daily shakha practice, yoga, and community service.
🌾
Rural Empowerment
Grassroots development reaching the most underserved communities across Bharat — from water conservation to livelihood creation.
